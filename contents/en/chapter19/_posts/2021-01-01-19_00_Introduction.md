Deep learning has transformed audio processing, enabling speech recognition, speaker identification, music generation, and audio synthesis. This chapter covers audio representation (spectrograms, MFCCs), speech recognition architectures (DeepSpeech, Whisper), text-to-speech (WaveNet, Tacotron), and music generation models.
